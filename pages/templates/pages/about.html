About Todd McCaffrey

I’m a novelist, technologist, and creative thinker — combining 50+ years of programming experience with a lifelong passion for storytelling. Whether I’m building scalable Django applications or publishing sci-fi and fantasy novels, I believe in shaping tools and tales that inspire.

I hold an MFA in Fine Arts and have authored multiple series under my name and pen names, including the L.A. Witch series and The Canaris Rift series.

As a developer, I work across Python, Django, and modern JavaScript stacks to create intuitive and efficient software — often blending tech and art.

When I’m not writing code or fiction, I explore AI, teach, and design systems that elevate human creativity.
